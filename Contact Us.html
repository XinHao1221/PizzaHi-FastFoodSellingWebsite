About Us

Pizza Hi featuring a delicious cuisine that includes the world’s favorite pan pizza with numerous crusts that suit the modern individuals' flavor. Other than that, signature dishes served as an important sides order that enable customers to have wider range of choice.

There are two different concepts: Pizza Hi Dine In & Pizza Hi Delivery. Pizza Hi Dine In offers a cozy & friendly atmosphere, which makes it the perfect place for one to relax, unwind & have a great time with family or friends. At the other hand, Pizza Hi Delivery provides guaranteed hot & fresh pizza within delivery time as promised.

Delivering more than hundreds of pizzas a day, Pizza Hi manage to compete with other pizza restaurants. Pizza Hi aims to become the world’s leading and fastest growing pizza delivery company. To archive this, product quality and operational excellence become the main factor to be considered by Pizza Hi.

Pizza Hi continues to strive towards delivering quality service and products to enhance customer satisfaction. By making use of modern technology, Pizza Hi is on its way to strike its sales performance by providing a precise, useful platform.
