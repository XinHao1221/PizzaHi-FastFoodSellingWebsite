FAQ

  1. What is Pizza Hi

    Pizza Hi is a food service provider that deliver various Italian cuisine, specifically pizza. Details can be viewed at here.

  2. How do I choose the pizza

    You may view the list of pizza by simply click on "Menu" at the navigation link above. Kindly select any pizza interested in the page.

  3. Where can I check the pizza choosen

    You may view the list of pizza chosen at "Add to Cart" the navigation link above.

  4. How can I track my order

    You can track your order at "Track Order" section.

  5. What can I do if I did not receive my pizza

    You may call 1-800-888-999 to enquire about your order.

  6. Is there any delivery charges

    The services is free of charge with minimum order or RM20.

  7. Can I cancel my order

    Once payment is made, no refund will be returned.

  8. Why there is a login button on top left of the page

    The button let user to sign in to their member account.

  9. How to become a member

    You may sign up at the login page. Follow the instructions and fill all the necessary information to sign up.

  10. Why becoming a member

    You will get updates on promos frequently. It lets you check your previous order history, deliver the best pizza preferences for you and more.

  11. Why the page is not responding

    This may cause by slow internet connection. You may try to refresh the page or wait for the page to respond. However, refreshing payment page is not recommended.

  12. Where can I submit my suggestions on the page

    You may feedback any suggestions here.
